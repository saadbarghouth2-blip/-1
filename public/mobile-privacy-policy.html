Riq Store Mobile Privacy Policy

This policy explains how the Riq Store mobile app handles customer information.

Information used by the app

The app may store cart contents, checkout details you type manually, and the location you choose for delivery confirmation.

Optional in-app account

If you choose to sign in with your email inside the mobile app, the app may store your email address, saved checkout details, and successful mobile-app order history in Supabase so future checkouts are faster and your past orders are easier to review.

Location usage

Location access is used only to help you pin the delivery address on the map and improve address accuracy during checkout.

Contact information

Name, phone number, email address, notes, and address data may be sent when you submit support requests or proceed to checkout.

Payments

Payments are completed through the current secure web checkout bridge. The app does not store card details locally.

Storage on device

The app stores cart and checkout draft data on your device so you can continue later. If you sign out, local cart data and checkout draft data are cleared from the app device storage.
