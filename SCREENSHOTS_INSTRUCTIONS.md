# Screenshots Instructions for App Store

## Required screenshot sizes (iPhone):
- 6.7-inch (iPhone Pro Max): 1284 × 2778 px (portrait)
- 6.5-inch: 1242 × 2688 px
- 5.5-inch: 1242 × 2208 px

## Recommended screenshots (5 images):
1. Home / storefront — show product grid and categories (use Arabic UI)
2. Product detail — show images, sizes, price, add-to-cart
3. Cart & Checkout — show address selection and fast checkout
4. Offers / Promotions — highlight daily deals
5. Order confirmation / Delivery tracking — show order status map or ETA

## Tips:
- Use Arabic captions short and bold
- Keep UI chrome minimal; focus on product & CTA
- Use device mockups for marketing but upload raw PNGs for App Store
- Provide at least 3-5 screenshots in Arabic and English variants if possible

## Filenames suggestion:
- screenshot-01-home-1284x2778.png
- screenshot-02-product-1284x2778.png
- screenshot-03-cart-1284x2778.png
- screenshot-04-offers-1284x2778.png
- screenshot-05-tracking-1284x2778.png

## Where to place screenshots in repo:
Add files to `mobile/assets/appstore-screenshots/` and share with marketing.
